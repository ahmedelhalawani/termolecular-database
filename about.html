About the Termolecular Reaction Database

The Termolecular Reaction Database is a curated collection of three-body reaction data designed for scientists, researchers, and students. It brings experimental results across a variety of sources, with an emphasis on accessibility.

Our goal is to provide a reliable resource that supports chemical kinetics, astrochemistry, atmospheric science, and fundamental studies of molecular processes.
