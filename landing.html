Why Termolecular Reactions Matter

Termolecular reactions play a vital role in atmospheric chemistry, combustion processes, and interstellar environments. This is a free and open source database that provides data on over 250 termolecular reactions across a variety of temperatures with proper sources provided.

Interesting Reactions in our Database

O + O + M → O₂ + M

Key for atmospheric oxygen formation. Data available across 200–1200 K.

H + O₂ + M → HO₂ + M

Critical in combustion reactions. Temperature-dependent rates included.

N + N + M → N₂ + M

Important for interstellar nitrogen balance. Data curated from multiple sources.

CH₃ + CH₃ + M → C₂H₆ + M

Central in combustion kinetics. Well-studied in lab conditions.
